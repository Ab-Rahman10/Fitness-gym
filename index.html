Here's how you will fit exercise into your daily routine

To fit exercise into your daily routine, start by scheduling short sessions, like 10-15 minutes, and gradually increase the duration. Incorporate physical activity into daily tasks, such as taking the stairs or walking during breaks. Vary your exercises to keep things interesting and work different muscle groups. Track your progress and consider finding a workout buddy or joining a class for motivation.
